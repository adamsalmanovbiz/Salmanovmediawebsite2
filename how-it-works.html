How It Works

The complete breakdown of our process, team, and what it takes to build distribution that compounds.

The Process

From Zero to Compounding Distribution

Most agencies talk about strategy. We build actual infrastructure. Here's exactly how we do it.

01

Onboarding & Discovery

We start by understanding your world. Your business, your audience, your goals, and what you're actually building.

  • Deep dive call to understand your story
  • Audit existing content (if any)
  • Define content pillars and messaging
  • Set up documentation systems
02

Content Capture Setup

We establish how content gets captured. Either we record ourselves, or we teach someone on your team (or you) to record, or a mix of both.

  • Equipment recommendations (if needed)
  • Recording workflows and templates
  • Weekly/monthly content capture schedule
  • Asynchronous communication system (Slack, voice notes, screen recordings)
03

Content Production

Raw footage and ideas become structured stories. Our team handles everything: editing, scripting, design, optimization.

  • Video editing (short-form & long-form)
  • Copywriting for captions and hooks
  • Graphic design and thumbnails
  • Platform-specific formatting (TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube)
04

Distribution & Publishing

Content goes live daily across all platforms. We handle scheduling, posting, and initial engagement.

  • Daily posting across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube
  • Optimized posting times per platform
  • Community management and engagement
  • Cross-platform repurposing
05

Optimization & Reporting

We analyze what works, what doesn't, and double down on winners. Content gets better every week.

  • Weekly performance analysis
  • A/B testing hooks, formats, and topics
  • Monthly strategy calls
  • Reporting on growth, reach, and engagement
The Team

The Content Team We Build For You

To do this yourself, you'd need to hire all of these roles. We handle it all as one cohesive system.

Content Strategist

Defines what you talk about, how you position yourself, and what stories to tell. Ensures everything ladders up to your goals.

Videographer

Captures the raw footage. Whether it's in-person shoots or teaching your team how to self-record with quality.

Video Editor

Turns raw footage into tight, engaging stories. Handles pacing, cuts, transitions, and all the technical work.

Copywriter

Writes hooks, captions, scripts, and CTAs. Makes sure the words hit as hard as the visuals.

Graphic Designer

Creates thumbnails, overlays, quote cards, and any visual assets needed to make content stand out.

Social Media Manager

Handles posting, scheduling, engagement, and community management. Keeps the machine running daily.

Creative Director

Oversees the entire operation. Ensures quality, consistency, and that everything aligns with your brand.

Performance Analyst

Tracks metrics, identifies winners, and optimizes strategy. Makes sure content improves week over week.

That's 8 specialized roles. You get all of them for less than one full-time hire.

Timeline

What to Expect

Content marketing is a compounding game. Here's the realistic timeline.

Week 1-2

Setup & Foundation

Onboarding, systems setup, content capture workflows established. First batch of content goes into production.

Week 3-4

Launch

Daily content starts publishing across platforms. Initial audience response and engagement tracking begins.

Month 2-3

Optimization

Data comes in. We identify what's working and double down. Content quality and performance improve week over week.

Month 4-6

Momentum

Distribution compounds. You start getting recognized. Inbound opportunities increase. The system runs smoothly in the background.

Month 6+

Leverage

Your presence is established. Can layer in lead magnets, offers, or ads on top of proven content. ROI becomes clear.
